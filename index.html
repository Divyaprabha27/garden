About Our Garden

Green Haven Arboretum is a 50-acre botanical paradise featuring over 2,000 plant species from around the world.

Our carefully curated collections showcase the diversity of plant life, from rare exotic specimens to native wildflowers. Walk through themed gardens, explore our tree collections, and experience the changing seasons in this living museum.
