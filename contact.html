Frequently Asked Questions

The garden is open daily from 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, with extended hours until 8:00 PM during summer months (June-August). Last entry is one hour before closing.

While walk-in visitors are welcome, we recommend booking tickets online during peak seasons and weekends to guarantee entry and skip the queue.
